Events And Festivals

MMMUT is host to several national, state-level conferences, seminars and programs . Apart from them, the college organises the following events and youth festivals:
Abhyudaya - national level cultural festival
TechSrijan - annual technical festival of the university organised with the help of the professional societies of SAE, IEEE, CES of the university
e-Yantra Robotics Competition - unique annual competition for undergraduate students in engineering, science, and polytechnic colleges
Avlamban - annual technical maga festival of the university organised by the civil engineering department of MMMUT
